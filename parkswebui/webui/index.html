Visiting Tips

  • Start from the back

    Begin your day from the far end of the park to avoid crowds.

  • Water attractions midday

    Visit Water World during the hottest part of the day (12PM-3PM).

  • Show schedule

    Plan your rides around the live shows to avoid missing performances.

  • Dining off-hours

    Eat lunch before 11:30AM or after 2PM to avoid restaurant crowds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Shankush Park and plan your visit with confidence.

FAQ Categories

Shankush Park is open 365 days a year (except on Dol Yatra) from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

During peak seasons and holidays, our hours may be extended. Please check our website or social media for any special timing announcements before your visit.

Shankush Park is centrally located and easily accessible by multiple modes of transportation:

  • By Car: Located off Highway 27, with ample parking available.
  • By Public Transport: Bus routes 34, 56, and 78 stop directly in front of the park.
  • By Metro: The nearest station is Central Park (Blue Line), a 5-minute walk to our main entrance.

You can find detailed directions and a map on our Contact page.

Yes, we have a large parking facility that can accommodate cars, buses, and motorcycles.

  • Regular parking: ₹100 per vehicle for the day
  • Premium parking (closer to entrance): ₹200 per vehicle
  • Bus parking: ₹300 per bus
  • Motorcycle parking: ₹50 per motorcycle

Annual pass holders receive free regular parking with their membership.

For the smallest crowds and shortest wait times, we recommend:

  • Weekdays (Tuesday-Thursday) generally have lower attendance
  • Early mornings (9:00 AM - 11:00 AM) or evenings (after 5:00 PM)
  • Non-holiday periods and outside of school vacation times

The park tends to be most crowded on weekends, holidays, and during summer vacation periods.

You can purchase tickets through multiple channels:

  • Online: Through our website's booking section (recommended for best prices and to avoid queues)
  • Mobile App: Download our Shankush Park app to purchase tickets and access exclusive offers
  • At the Park: Ticket counters are available at the main entrance
  • Authorized Retailers: Select travel agencies and partner hotels

We recommend purchasing tickets online in advance to secure your preferred date and avoid potential sellouts, especially during peak seasons.

We offer several ticket options to suit different needs:

  • Individual Entry: Basic admission with access to all standard attractions
  • Entry + Meal Package: Includes entry and a meal voucher redeemable at park restaurants
  • Family Pack: Discounted bundle for families of 4-6 people
  • Group Packages: Special rates for groups of 10 or more people
  • Annual Passes: Unlimited visits throughout the year with special perks
  • Special Event Tickets: For seasonal celebrations and events

Visit our Ticket Booking section for current pricing and package details.

Yes, we offer special pricing for various groups:

  • Children: Kids under 3 years enter free; ages 3-12 receive reduced pricing
  • Seniors: Visitors aged 65+ receive a 20% discount on all ticket types
  • Students: Valid student ID holders get a 15% discount on individual tickets
  • Military Personnel: Active and retired military receive a 25% discount with valid ID
  • Differently-abled Visitors: Special rates available with appropriate documentation
  • School Groups: Special educational packages for schools with advance booking

Please note that valid identification may be required at the entrance to verify eligibility for discounts.

Our ticket policies regarding cancellations and rescheduling are as follows:

  • Tickets can be rescheduled up to 48 hours before your visit date at no extra charge
  • Cancellations made 7+ days before visit date: 100% refund
  • Cancellations made 3-6 days before visit date: 75% refund
  • Cancellations made 48-72 hours before visit date: 50% refund
  • Cancellations less than 48 hours before visit date: No refund

For rescheduling or cancellation, please contact our customer service team or manage your booking through your online account.

In case of park closure due to severe weather or unforeseen circumstances, we offer full refunds or rescheduling options.

Yes, many rides have height and/or age restrictions for safety reasons:

  • Thrill rides (like Cyclone): Minimum height of 140cm and age 12+
  • Water slides: Minimum height of 120cm, children under 10 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Family rides: Most require children under 8 to be accompanied by an adult
  • Kids Zone: Designed for children under 8 years, with a maximum height of 120cm for some attractions

Height measurement stations are available throughout the park, and all rides have clear signage indicating restrictions. Our staff can help determine which rides are suitable for your group.

We strictly enforce these restrictions for everyone's safety.

For maximum enjoyment of our Water World area and water rides, we recommend bringing:

  • Swimwear (proper swimming attire is required, no street clothes allowed on water slides)
  • Towels (also available for rent)
  • Waterproof sunscreen
  • Flip-flops or water shoes
  • Change of clothes
  • Waterproof phone case if you wish to take photos

We provide complimentary lockers near the water attractions to store your belongings securely. Changing rooms and showers are also available.

For safety reasons, eyeglasses should be secured with straps, and loose items like jewelry should be stored in lockers.

Wait times vary depending on the day, time, and season:

  • On weekdays during off-peak season: 5-15 minutes for most rides
  • On weekends and during school holidays: 15-45 minutes for popular attractions
  • During peak summer season and holidays: 30-60 minutes for the most popular rides

You can check real-time wait times on our mobile app, which we highly recommend downloading before your visit. The app also shows a heat map of the park to help you plan your day more efficiently.

For shorter wait times, we recommend using the "single rider" lines (if you don't mind being seated separately from your group) or visiting popular attractions during showtimes or during lunch/dinner hours.

Shankush Park offers a variety of dining options to suit all tastes and budgets:

  • Beach Café: Casual dining with sandwiches, salads, and light meals
  • Pizza Planet: Family-friendly pizzeria with pasta options
  • Thrill Grill: Burgers, grilled items, and hearty meals
  • Vista Restaurant: Premium dining with table service and a varied menu
  • Spice Route: Indian and Asian cuisine
  • Quick Bites: Various snack stands throughout the park offering ice cream, popcorn, pretzels, and more
  • Coffee Shop: Hot beverages, pastries, and light snacks

Most restaurants can accommodate special dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform staff about any allergies or specific needs.

You can also pre-purchase meal vouchers or dining packages when booking your tickets for convenience and savings.

Yes, Shankush Park has comprehensive medical facilities for visitor safety:

  • Three First Aid stations located throughout the park, clearly marked on park maps
  • Trained medical staff on duty whenever the park is open
  • Capability to handle minor injuries and health concerns
  • Emergency response protocols for more serious situations
  • AEDs (Automated External Defibrillators) stationed at key locations

The main First Aid center is located near the park entrance, with satellite stations in the Water World area and Thrill Zone.

If you have specific medical concerns or require medication storage (like insulin refrigeration), please speak with our First Aid staff upon arrival.

Yes, Shankush Park is committed to being accessible and enjoyable for all guests:

  • Wheelchair accessibility throughout most of the park
  • Accessible restrooms at all restroom locations
  • Wheelchair rentals available at Guest Services
  • Service animal relief areas
  • Special boarding procedures for most rides
  • Sensory guides indicating sound, light, and motion levels for each attraction
  • Quiet rooms for guests who need a break from sensory stimulation
  • Companion restrooms

We recommend visiting our Guest Services desk upon arrival to receive a Accessibility Guide and personalized assistance for your visit.

Please note that some attractions have restrictions based on the nature of the experience, but our staff will help identify suitable alternatives.

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout Shankush Park:

  • Connect by accepting terms and conditions on the login page

Booking a group visit is simple:

  1. Initial Contact: Complete our Group Inquiry Form on the website, email groups@Shankush park.com, or call our Group Booking line at 1800-2030-090
  2. Consultation: Our group coordinator will discuss your requirements, recommend suitable packages, and provide a detailed quote
  3. Reservation: Once you're ready to proceed, we'll send a booking form and require a deposit (typically 25%) to secure your date
  4. Planning: For larger groups and special events, we'll schedule a planning meeting or call to finalize all details
  5. Final Payment: The remaining balance is due 7 days before your visit date
  6. Visit Day: Arrive at our dedicated group entrance where your coordinator will meet you

For school groups, we offer educational materials in advance to help teachers prepare students for the visit.

Group bookings should be made at least 2 weeks in advance, longer for peak seasons and special events. For groups of 50+, we recommend booking 1-2 months ahead.
